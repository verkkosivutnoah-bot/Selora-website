# Selora — Paid Ads Campaign Brief

## Campaign objective
Drive Finnish SMB owners to book a free 30-min demo call. Secondary: blog reads + email subscribers as warm-up.

## Funnel
**Cold** (Meta / TikTok / YouTube) → land on `index.html#demo` → see live demo → click "Varaa ilmainen demo" → Calendly.
**Warm** (Google search / LinkedIn) → land on `tekoalyvastaanottajat.html` or pricing → contact form.

## Messaging pillars

### Pillar 1 — Lost calls = lost revenue
Hook: "Kuinka monta puhelua menetit tällä viikolla?" / "How many calls did you miss this week?"
Angle: ROI math. Concrete numbers. 78 % recovery stat.

### Pillar 2 — Always on, never sick
Hook: "Vastaa 24/7. Ei sairaslomia."
Angle: Reliability. Solo entrepreneur pain. Picture: empty repair shop phone ringing.

### Pillar 3 — Sounds Finnish, sounds human
Hook: "Suomenkielinen tekoäly. Asiakkaat eivät huomaa."
Angle: Quality + native language differentiator vs international AI tools.

### Pillar 4 — 48 hour go-live
Hook: "Käyttöönotto alle 48 tunnissa."
Angle: Speed. No long IT project. Owner-led.

### Pillar 5 — Industry specific
Hook variants per industry: "Kampaamosi puhelin", "Korjaamosi puhelin", "Ravintolasi puhelin"
Angle: Recognition. Industry-tailored pages.

## Concepts

### Concept A — Lost Call ROI (Pillar 1)

**Format**: Bold typographic stat over dark blue aurora gradient bg, brand badge top-left, CTA bottom.
**Sizes**: Meta feed 1080×1350, Reels/TikTok 1080×1920, Google 1200×628, LinkedIn 1080×1080, YouTube 1280×720.

**Visual recipe**:
- Background: `#060c18` with radial blue aurora `rgba(29,78,216,0.45)` from top
- Top-left: gradient logo dot 36×36 + "SELORA" wordmark (Inter 600, letter-spacing 0.06em, white)
- Center-stage: massive number "+34 %" or "78 %" in Inter 800, gradient silver clip-text, drop-shadow
- Below: Inter 500 white "enemmän varauksia / more bookings" small caps eyebrow + statement
- Bottom: pill CTA "Varaa demo →" (white pill, dark text, soft shadow)

**Copy options (FI / EN)**:
- "Saa 78 % menetetyistä puheluista takaisin." / "Recover 78% of missed calls."
- "+34 % varauksia. Kuukauden kuluttua käyttöönotosta." / "+34% bookings. Within a month of going live."
- "Yksi puhelu = yksi asiakas. Älä menetä yhtäkään." / "One call = one customer. Don't miss any."

### Concept B — Phone is ringing, you're working (Pillar 2)

**Format**: Split: left half = phone ringing (warm tone, slight glow), right half = product UI mock (Selora dashboard fragment) on dark.
**Sizes**: Meta 1080×1350, LinkedIn 1080×1080, Google 1200×628.

**Visual recipe**:
- Left 50%: dark navy with iPhone shape ringing (3 concentric pulse rings)
- Right 50%: card with call log row: green dot + "Vastattu — 12:43" + transcript snippet
- Bridging line: subtle gradient stroke connects the two halves
- Top-right: Selora logo
- Bottom: "Selora vastaa, sinä työskentelet." / "Selora answers. You work."

### Concept C — Industry recognition (Pillar 5)

**Format**: Industry photo (real, not stock-y) overlaid with Selora dark gradient + question hook.
**Sizes**: Meta 1080×1350, TikTok 1080×1920.

Variants:
- Kampaamo / Hair salon
- Autokorjaamo / Auto repair
- Ravintola / Restaurant

**Visual recipe**:
- Full bleed industry photo (low saturation, blue color grade)
- Bottom 40%: dark `#060c18` gradient overlay fade
- Headline (Inter 300/Instrument Serif italic mix): "Kampaamosi puhelin — kuka vastaa kun olet leikkaamassa?"
- Sub: "Selora vastaa puolestasi. 24/7."

### Concept D — Side-by-side: human vs AI (Pillar 3)

**Format**: Two-column comparison card.
**Sizes**: LinkedIn 1080×1080, Google 1200×628.

**Visual recipe**:
- Left col: "Vastaanottaja" — €3 200 / kk, 8h/päivä, lomat, sairaslomat
- Right col: "Selora" — alkaen 490€ / kk, 24/7, ei lomia, suomeksi
- Center divider: vertical line + "vs" badge
- Top: eyebrow "Vertailu"
- Bottom CTA: "Varaa demo, näe miten kuulostaa."

### Concept E — 48h timeline (Pillar 4)

**Format**: Horizontal timeline, 4 steps animated reveal (static for image).
**Sizes**: Google 1200×628, YouTube 1280×720.

**Visual recipe**:
- 4 numbered circles (gradient blue) on horizontal line: 01 Kartoitus → 02 Persoonan luonti → 03 Integrointi → 04 Live
- Below: "48 h yhteensä. Sinä keskityt asiakkaisiin." / "48 hours total. You focus on customers."
