# SnapPlate — Design Notes

> Captures the product thinking behind onboarding, gamification, and the viral mechanism. Source-of-truth for design decisions before they land in code.

---

## The unique angle

The strongest differentiator is **an AI coach persona generated from a questionnaire**. Five distinct vibes (drill sergeant, hype bestie, zen monk, pragmatic trainer, roastmaster). Every notification, meal critique, and workout nudge is in-character. Same data, five completely different apps.

Pair it with a **single visible game loop** — your meal photos + workouts feed XP into one "physique avatar" that visibly levels up — rather than scattered streaks/badges/leaderboards.

**Key tradeoff:** doubling down on personality + one gamified metric is more memorable and shareable, but it commits us to nailing the AI voice (boring coach = dead app), whereas a generic "calories + workouts + streaks" combo is safer but indistinguishable from Cal AI, MyFitnessPal, or Fitbod.

---

## Onboarding questionnaire (~90 sec, 6 questions)

1. **Goal** — lose / maintain / gain
2. **Coach vibe** — pick 1 of 5 personas (Sgt. Steel / Maya / Coach Dana / Roshi / The Goblin)
3. **Tone tolerance** — slider: "be gentle" ↔ "roast me"
4. **When are you most likely to skip the gym?** — morning / after work / weekends / when stressed (drives smart-timed notifications)
5. **What's your kryptonite food?** — free text (the AI references this in nudges)
6. **In 90 days, success looks like ___** — single sentence, becomes lock-screen banner

The Goblin is gated past the midpoint of the tone slider, with a two-tap opt-in confirmation.

## Gamification

### Primary loop (ship at launch)

1. **Physique avatar.** One 3D-ish character that visibly evolves with real progress. Hit protein 7 days running → it gets visibly leaner / more defined. Skip a week → it slumps. **No numbers, just vibes.**
2. **3 daily quests.** Generated by AI from the user's goal: e.g. "Hit 150g protein", "Walk 8k steps", "Sleep before midnight". Complete all 3 = **"Perfect Day"** combo multiplier (2× XP).
3. **Coach affinity.** Relationship with the chosen coach levels up. Higher levels unlock new coach lines, voice messages, harder challenges. Makes the persona feel earned.

### Layer in post-launch

4. **Boss fights.** A "Pizza Friday" boss appears Friday 6pm. Log a sub-700-cal dinner → defeated, cosmetic loot drops. Skipping = avatar takes cosmetic damage (never punishing, never shaming).
5. **Loot drops.** Quest completion drops cosmetic gear for the avatar (gym fits, shoes, hats). Some only unlock above 30-day streaks.
6. **Seasons.** 12-week arcs ending in a before/after reveal collage that's screenshot-perfect for socials. Each season has a theme ("Cut Season", "Bulk Arc").
7. **Duo mode.** Pair with one friend. Your avatars stand side-by-side on the home screen. Their avatar visibly notices when you skip. **Soft accountability — no leaderboards.** (Leaderboards burn out fast.)
8. **Photo-quality XP scaling.** Better-framed meal photos = more XP. Encourages the core action (logging) without nagging.

### Aesthetic guardrail

Gamification can feel infantilizing for adults if the visuals look like Duolingo. Aim for **Apple Fitness / Nike Training Club aesthetic with RPG mechanics underneath** — minimalist, premium, but the systems are deep. Risk: under-design it = toy. Over-design it = work.

## The viral mechanism (TikTok loop)

Funny lines aren't enough. **Build the screenshot loop into the app:**

1. **One-tap "Share the roast"** on every notification — exports a perfectly-framed image: meal photo + coach line + tiny SnapPlate watermark. Optimized for the 9:16 TikTok / IG story crop. Zero editing required.
2. **Weekly recap auto-video** — generates a 15–20 sec vertical video at the end of each week: 5 of your worst logs + coach's funniest reactions, stitched with a beat. One tap → drafts to TikTok / IG.
3. **Hype-mode unlock cinematic** — the rare moment any coach goes sincere is auto-recorded as a 6-sec shareable clip with confetti. Players grind 30+ days for this.
4. **"Drag me" prompt** — once a week, user can submit a meal manually and pay (in-app currency or Pro) for The Goblin to roast it on demand. TikTok format primed: *"i let my fitness app roast my dinner."*

**Structural insight:** don't hope users screenshot — design the screenshot for them, then make sharing one tap. TikTok virality isn't a marketing afterthought, it's the **primary acquisition channel**, so the artifact has to be perfect at the source.

## Coach affinity mechanic (per-coach detail)

| Coach | Affinity reward at max |
|---|---|
| Sgt. Steel | Unlocks "field promotion" — a one-time clip where he calls you a soldier, not a recruit. |
| Maya | Unlocks "BFF mode" — voice notes and surprise hype texts on rough days. |
| Coach Dana | Unlocks "advanced analytics" view — predictive insights normally locked behind Pro. |
| Roshi | Unlocks a new metaphor library + a "morning sit" guided breath ritual. |
| The Goblin | Unlocks "Mode: Hype" — one (1) sincere compliment, recorded as a shareable cinematic clip. |

The Goblin's hype-mode is the most coveted because his is the rarest break in character. People will grind to see it.

## Pricing & paywall

Currently mocked as:
- **Monthly:** $9.99/mo
- **Yearly:** $39.99/yr (BEST VALUE — Save 67%, default selected)
- **Trial:** 7-day free
- **CTA:** "Start 7-day free trial" → "Then $39.99/year. Cancel anytime."

Free tier limit (TBD): probably 5 AI scans per week + 1 coach (Maya by default). Pro unlocks all 5 coaches, unlimited scans, advanced macros, custom goals, "drag me" feature, export.

The Goblin and any coach-affinity rewards are Pro-only — that's the upgrade hook.

## Risk register

- **Slang rots fast.** TikTok-current vocabulary is cringe in 6 months. The Goblin needs a quarterly slang refresh + denylist (see `coaches/goblin.md`).
- **Eating-disorder territory.** Any coach making behavior comments needs a hardcoded forbidden-topics filter. No body / weight / appearance / restriction language — ever. Behavior > identity, always.
- **Coach voice drift.** LLM-generated lines can drift off-persona. Voice doctrine + sample lines + few-shot prompts are load-bearing. Build a "this is off-brand" feedback path for every notification.
- **Notification fatigue.** Too many = uninstall. Throttle hard: max 3 coach messages/day default, user-tunable.
- **Privacy.** Meal photos are sensitive (location, faces, food preferences). Default to local processing where feasible, never share with third-party analytics raw.
