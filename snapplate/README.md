# SnapPlate

> AI-powered meal tracking + fitness motivation app. Snap a photo → AI returns calories and macros → personalized AI coach keeps you accountable.

---

## Concept

The core loop:

> **Photo → AI analysis → daily nutrition tracking → AI coach reaction.**

What makes SnapPlate different from Cal AI / MyFitnessPal isn't the calorie tracking — it's the **AI coach persona** the user picks during onboarding. Five distinct coaches, each with a unique voice, drive every notification, meal critique, and fitness nudge in-character. Same data, five completely different apps.

---

## The 5 coach personas

The questionnaire-generated coach is the moat. See [`coaches/`](./coaches/) for full specs.

| Coach | Vibe | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **Sgt. Steel** | Drill Sergeant — discipline, structure, military framing | Type-A users who want accountability |
| **Maya** | Hype Bestie — pure positivity, anti-shame | Burnout-prone users, wellness-leaning |
| **Coach Dana** | Pragmatic Trainer — data-driven, peer-not-parent | Engineers, data nerds |
| **Roshi** | Zen Monk — mindful, metaphor, anti-restriction | Mindfulness folks, anti-burnout |
| **The Goblin** 🔒 | Roastmaster — Gen Z comedian, makes fun of you | Gen Z, viral / TikTok crowd (opt-in only) |

The Goblin is locked behind a tone-slider opt-in past "be gentle" with a two-tap confirm.

## Gamification

Single primary loop at launch:

1. **Physique avatar** — one 3D-ish character that visibly evolves with real progress
2. **3 daily quests** generated by the AI from your goal (e.g. "Hit 150g protein", "Walk 8k", "Sleep before midnight"). All 3 done = "Perfect Day" combo multiplier.
3. **Coach affinity** — your relationship with the chosen coach levels up; higher levels unlock new lines, voice messages, harder challenges.

Layered post-launch: boss fights (Pizza Friday), cosmetic loot drops, 12-week seasons with before/after reveal collages, duo/guild mode, photo-quality XP scaling.

See [`design-notes.md`](./design-notes.md) for full breakdown.

## Onboarding questionnaire (~90 sec)

1. **Goal** — lose / maintain / gain
2. **Coach vibe** — pick 1 of 5 personas
3. **Tone tolerance** — slider: "be gentle" ↔ "roast me" (gates The Goblin past midpoint)
4. **When are you most likely to skip the gym?** — morning / after work / weekends / when stressed
5. **What's your kryptonite food?** — free text (the AI references this in nudges)
6. **In 90 days, success looks like ___** — single sentence, becomes lock-screen banner

---

## Viral mechanism (TikTok loop)

Built-in screenshot/share infrastructure:

1. **One-tap "Share the roast"** on every notification — exports a 9:16 image with notification + meal photo + watermark, optimized for TikTok / IG story crop.
2. **Weekly "Goblin Reads My Diet" recap** — auto-generates a 15–20 sec vertical video stitching your worst logs + coach reactions to a beat. One tap → drafts to TikTok / IG.
3. **Hype-mode unlock cinematic** — when you reach max coach affinity, the rare sincere line is auto-recorded as a 6-sec shareable clip with confetti.
4. **"Drag me" prompt** — once a week, user can submit a meal manually for an on-demand roast (Pro / in-app currency).

The app makes its own marketing content.

## Design system

**Colors:**
- Primary: `#10B981` (brand green — health, freshness, "go")
- Accent: `#F97316` (warm orange — calories, food, energy)
- Background: `#F9FAFB` (very light gray)
- Text: `#111827` (near-black) / `#64748B` (muted)
- Macro: protein blue `#3B82F6`, carbs amber `#F59E0B`, fat rose `#F43F5E`

**Typography:** Inter (300/400/500/600/700/800). System fallback.

**Tokens:** rounded-2xl on cards, rounded-full on buttons, generous spacing, min 44px tap targets, subtle shadows, no heavy borders.

**Aesthetic target:** Apple Fitness / Nike Training Club premium — RPG mechanics underneath, never Duolingo cartoon.
